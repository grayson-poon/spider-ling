About & Credits:

Spidey has been captured and needs to escape this EVIL lair. Good thing he has YOU, his person in the chair!
Help Spidey navigate through the tunnels, cooridors, and passageways of wherever he is and get him back to New York City so he can continue protecting the neighborhood!

Beware of TRAPS that Spidey could run into.
Sprite images belong to Angry Clown Entertainment. Check them out here!
Controls:
  • Use keyboard keys "A" and "D" to guide Spidey left/right
  • Press the spacebar to jump
  • Use the mouse/trackpad and click anywhere on the game view to launch Spidey in that direction!
  • Spidey can only boost two times in a row before needing to catch his breath and rest on the ground, so use the boosts wisely!
How to play:
  • Guide Spidey to the light-blue zones on the screen (these will take you to the next level)
  • Avoid making Spidey touch any orange zones (traps)
  • With great power comes great responsibility. Play responsibly! :)
